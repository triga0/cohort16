MY REFACTORY JOURNEY

Week 1 , 2: Personal Development

Class Overview:

The first week of the Personal Development course focused on self-awareness and goal setting. The aim was to help students identify their strengths and weaknesses and set achievable personal and professional goals.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Self-Awareness:
    • Techniques for self-assessment, such as SWOT analysis.
    • The importance of emotional intelligence in personal development.
  • Goal Setting:
    • SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) goals.
    • Short-term vs. long-term goals.
  • Personal Growth Strategies:
    • Building a growth mindset.
    • Techniques for continuous learning and self-improvement.

    Week 1 , 2: Introduction to Software Development

Class Overview:

The second week of the Leadership course focused on communication and conflict resolution. Effective communication is crucial for leaders to convey their vision and resolve conflicts within their teams.


Key Topics Covered:

    • SDLC Models: Waterfall, Agile, Scrum.
    • Programming Basics: Variables, data types, control structures, functions.
    • Version Control: Introduction to Git and GitHub.
